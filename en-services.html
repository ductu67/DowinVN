PROCESS

Standard Installation Process

4 clear steps — customers know exactly what happens at each stage

1
Site Survey & Consultation

A DOWIN engineer surveys the site (in person or via drawings/video). Assessment covers power supply, compressed air, foundations and installation space. The most suitable model and configuration is recommended.

1–2 working days
2
Contract & Preparation

Technical specifications, warranty terms and delivery schedule are confirmed. Customer prepares foundations to DOWIN-supplied drawings. DOWIN orders and quality-inspects the machine before dispatch.

7–21 days (depending on model)
3
Installation & Trial Run

DOWIN technicians install the machine, connect electrical wiring and compressed air, and configure PLC parameters. No-load and loaded trials are run with real sand. Calibration continues until output and mold quality meet specification.

3–5 days (single machine) / 7–14 days (production line)
4
Training & Acceptance

Operating team is trained on-site. Technical manuals, wiring diagrams and operating instructions are handed over in English. Acceptance certificate is signed and the 12-month warranty period begins.

2 days training
