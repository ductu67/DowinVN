About DOWIN Vietnam

ABOUT US

A Pioneer in Industrial Casting

Founded in 2011, DOWIN Vietnam specialises in the research, manufacturing and distribution of high-quality industrial casting equipment. With over 15 years of experience, we have become the trusted partner of hundreds of foundries and metalworking factories across Vietnam.

Our DW-series green sand molding machines are designed and manufactured in Vietnam, combining advanced servo technology with designs optimised for real-world production conditions in Vietnamese foundries.

OUR HISTORY

Our Journey

  • 2011
    Founded
    Started in Bac Ninh

    DOWIN Vietnam was founded by a team of mechanical engineers specialising in casting. The first production workshop was established in Bac Ninh, with initial products being manual sand molding machines.

  • 2015
    DW Series Launch
    DW-1000 product line developed

    After 4 years of R&D, the DW-1000 with servo control system was launched. Market response exceeded expectations — 50 orders in the first year.

  • 2018
    Product Range Expansion
    DW-1200, DW-1600 and Sand Mixer launched

    Completed the casting equipment ecosystem with the DW-1200 for the mid-range segment, DW-1600 for large-scale operations, and the automated sand mixer.

  • 2021
    Integrated Lines
    Fully automated casting line solution launched

    For the first time, a complete automated casting line solution was offered — from sand mixing and mold making to conveyor systems and product sorting.

  • 2024
    Today
    500+ machines operating nationwide

    Over 500 machines are now operating in foundries from north to south Vietnam. DOWIN continues to expand its network of technicians and distributors nationwide.
