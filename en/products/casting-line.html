LINE PROCESS

6-Step Closed Loop

Full automation of the entire sand casting process from start to finish

Step 01

Sand Preparation

Automatic sand mixer blends sand, bentonite and water at optimal ratios with continuous moisture monitoring.

Step 02

Molding

DW molding machine automatically produces molds with high precision, 15–30 second cycle time.

Step 03

Core Setting

Automated core handling system (or manual, depending on configuration) places cores precisely into molds.

Step 04

Metal Pouring

Automatic pouring ladle or temperature-controlled trough ensures precise metal volume per mold.

Step 05

Cooling

Variable-speed cooling conveyor ensures metal cools evenly, preventing cracks and defects.

Step 06

Shakeout & Sand Recovery

Shakeout machine, sand screening and recovery system recycles used sand to reduce material costs.
